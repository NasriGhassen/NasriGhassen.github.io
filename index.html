What is Web Design for me?

My story started since I was 11. I used to make PowerPoint and call
them Websites. That was before I embark on my current journey
to learn the techologies behind acheiving a good looking and an
effective Web Design.
I tried to learn the basics HTML, CSS and PHP by myself and then
support it with what I learned in my studies. I learned .NET and J2EE
and currently use MEAN Stack in my school projects.
I've always had the vision of combining art and technology, so this,
for me is an opportunity to fulfill that.
Feel free to explore my humble Skills and Knowledge
in this domain.

A push in the right direction.

I've always been a good Designer, at first it was a struggle
since people around me didn't understand my ambitions and
they kept dragging me down.
With the support of my parents I got into an Art high school
I felt like something bloomed in my mind and I had to reach my goal.
My last year in El Omrane I, along a few of my class mates,
got invited to the "Students' Spring Festival" in Nice - France.
We competed with the Mediterranean countries there and we nailed
the first prize in the Art and Painting Competition. the experience
was as enriching as it sounds like.

From Physical to Digital

The more I learned about Design the more I wanted to switch
to digital art. I got myself a graphic tablet and started practicing
The more I worked with it the more I became comfortable using it
Digital Art made me feel like it was what I intended to do.
I started working with the Adobe Collection and it only
made me ambitious. It helped me a lot in Web Design and Graphic creations.
I Design my own content I use in the Websites, from Logos
and Buttons to Illustrations and Artwork.

Future Vision

Other than Frontend development I am also a Backend developper but I want
side of the Web Design, since I can put so much work on it and try to
come up with innovative ways in making the designs.
I am also trying to implement new technologies in Web Design like AR and VR.

Soft Skills

I developped a sense of consciousness and awareness when communicating
with others, I tend to be energetic and direct when adressing my team mates.
I am also learning several languages because in my opinion it is the only
border that limits cultural and professional integration.

About Me

Hi ! I'm Ghassan Nasri

I am a 23 years old student currently pursuing my dream in mastering Web Development. I am mainly a painter and a sculpter but since I was a little kid I always dreamt of combining Art and Technology. I decided to achieve my vision through my cursus for now until it becomes more independent. I am really passionate about Web Design, punctual and respectful.

I am really fond of learning languages and currently speak 3 fluently, Arabic, English and French and learning Norwegian. I am also fond of psychology which helps the individual to be aware of his environment and gives him the tools to interact with it properly.

I got inspired to follow up the Web Design as a career as it is an intriguing experience in which I feel free and able to deliver a good content.
